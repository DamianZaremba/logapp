Overview

LogApp is a logging application aimed at people who enjoy the outdoors.

Whether you're required to keep logs for permits/awards or just want to track progress LogApp has something to offer.

Currently we support the following log types:

  • Indoor/Trad Climbing

Who?

Damian is a Systems Administrator working for Sub6

Outside of work he is a keen Climbing Instructor, Scout leader, walker and generally likes being outdoors.

When out doing group/solo work he likes to keep logs as evidence for permits and NGB awards.

As a hacker interested in open source and manipulating data, paper based logging was very limiting (and paper gets ruined very easily).

To allow a better use of the data and as a permanent record he created LogApp, a Django based logging system.

Features
  • GPL licensed - Take it, hack it, contribute to it and make the world a better place.
  • Secure - All traffic is encrypted via SSL, data is stored in a secured database, passwords are encrypted.
  • PDF exporting - Create professional looking diaries.
  • Social integration - Hook your account up to twitter and let everyone know what you've been up to.
  • Embeddable widgets - Share mapped routes on your site.
